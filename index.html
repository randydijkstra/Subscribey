About Subscribey

Subscribey is a Spotify app developed by Mick van Dijk and Randy Dijkstra as a schoolproject for Hogeschool Rotterdam.

Subscribey has a simple purpose of letting you subscribe to an album as a playlist, of the current song you are listening to!

Functionalities

In our applicaties we have multiple functions. We are getting information out of Spotify and Last.FM. You can find the following functions in the app:

  • Tabs
  • Last.FM:
    • Get Similar artists
    • Get Topalbums
    • Get Toptracks
  • Track info
  • Playlist
  • Subscribe
  • Share
